About Blog of the .Day

While most of your experience of the web may be with corporate-owned websites, there are many people bucking this trend with a rebellious act: creating and maintaining a personal website.

Bloggers around the world talk about the consequential and the inconsequential, from one's failed attempt to make a recipe to an idea that challenges the way we think about social networking.

Every day, Blog of the .Day highlights a new blog. The blog is chosen at random and a new one should be deployed daily via Netlify. note: in May 2024 Joe Crawford became the steward of this project.

Want to add your blog to the list? Submit a pull request on the project GitHub page.
